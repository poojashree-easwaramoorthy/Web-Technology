About Vivo Mobiles

Founded in 2009, Vivo is a global technology company that designs, develops, and manufactures smartphones and smartphone accessories, software, and online services. With a vision to create technology for a better world, Vivo continually invests in cutting-edge technology and innovation.

With a dedicated team of engineers, designers, and marketers, Vivo has achieved numerous milestones, including the development of the world's first smartphone with a built-in fingerprint scanner and the launch of the first 5G-enabled smartphone.
